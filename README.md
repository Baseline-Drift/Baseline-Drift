BASELINE DRIFT // STATUS HUD

> A self-monitoring tool built from a neurodivergent perspective.

---

// WHAT IS THIS

Baseline Drift is a personal status tracker for people whose internal state isn't always easy to read, communicate, or remember to check.

It gives you four sliders — Vitality, Stamina, Mood, and Focus — and asks you to pick a band that fits, once a day. Not a precise number. Not a clinical score. Just an honest read of where you're at right now.

Set your sliders. Log your day. See your patterns.


---

// WHO IT'S FOR

Built from a neurodivergent perspective. Useful for anyone who:

Struggles to identify or name their internal states in real time

Has days where functioning is hard and wants an honest record of that

Wants to see patterns in their energy, mood, and focus over time without a clinical framework

Needs something that meets them where they are — including on the low days



---

// HOW IT WORKS

Each stat has five bands:

Band	Range	Meaning

LOW	0–25	Significantly depleted
BELOW CENTRE	26–49	Below your baseline
CENTRE	50	Right at your baseline
ABOVE CENTRE	51–75	Above your baseline
HIGH	76–100	Strong capacity


Pick the band that fits. Don’t overthink the number.

Your personal baseline is configurable — if your functional centre sits at 40 rather than 50, the condition thresholds shift to match you.

> Baseline is personal and does not represent a universal norm.




---

// CONDITIONS

The app reads your overall state from your combined stats:

Condition	Meaning

OPTIMAL	All systems nominal
FUNCTIONAL	Operational — mind your limits
BASELINE	You're here, that's enough
CRITICAL	System stressed — survive mode
DYSREGULATED	High variance — body and mind out of sync


DYSREGULATED fires when your stats show significant spread — for example, VITALITY critically low while FOCUS is high. This is a recognised state, not a failure.

> Conditions are heuristic interpretations of your inputs, not objective or clinical states.

// FEATURES

Daily logging with optional notes — capture context behind the numbers

Trend graph with date range filters (7D / 30D / 3M / 6M / 12M / ALL)

RECALL LAST — reload your previous reading into the sliders

USE AS-IS — confirm current slider positions without movement

Stat locks — hold a stat in place during reset or recall

BOOST button — customisable +5 to all unlocked stats (default: ☕ COFFEE)

Personal baseline calibration — conditions relative to your centre, not a fixed scale

Note to self — a grounding anchor displayed on the main HUD

Backup & restore — full JSON export of settings and history

Colourblind mode — high-contrast accessible palette

Three font options — Atkinson Hyperlegible (default), Share Tech Mono, OpenDyslexic

Emergency contacts — shown in CRITICAL state

In-app changelog — tap the version number in settings

Full feature guide — tap ? HELP on the main screen


> BOOST is a manual adjustment, not a measured or automatic change.

// TECH

Single-file HTML application. No framework. No database. No server.

All data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage). Nothing is sent anywhere. Export your data regularly using the BACKUP feature to protect against cache clears.
