// FEATURE GUIDE
MAIN HUD
Baseline Drift is a translation layer — it converts your internal state into something visible and trackable. Set sliders to where you actually are, not where you want to be. The app shows patterns. You interpret them.
Tap your player name to edit it inline (max 12 characters)
Tap SET YOUR STATS to view the band reference table
Tap ◈ BRAIN STATE next to the condition badge to open the AuADHD flow
→ Identifies your current cognitive pattern (Aligned, Friction, Override, Stuck, Depletion) and suggests a practical lever
RECALL LAST loads your most recent logged reading into the sliders
USE AS-IS confirms current slider positions without movement
Tap the lock icon 🔓 on any stat to hold it
→ Locked stats are not affected by reset or recall
Add an optional note when logging to capture context behind the numbers
DYSREGULATED (Mild or Severe) triggers when any two stats differ by ≥40 points
→ Body and mind out of sync — this is a recognised state, not a failure
SHUTDOWN triggers when all sliders are ≤25 — system collapse, survival mode only
FLOW STATE triggers when Focus ≥85 and Vitality ≥40 — stable high-attention dominance
GROUNDED triggers when all stats are between 45–65 — stable and centred, no peaks or valleys
LOCKED IN triggers when Focus ≥75 and all stats ≥50 — full coherent functioning
All values are self-rated and subjective. Focus on relative state, not exact numbers.
Drift detection monitors your stats against your personal baselines — if two or more stats drift significantly, a nudge appears on the main HUD. Tap 1H, 4H, or TOMORROW to snooze it.
Tap ▶ SNAPSHOT to expand a summary of current stat values, total, trend direction, and condition status. These numbers are informational only.
COMMUNITY
Tap ▶ COMMUNITY in the footer to access SHARE, TIP via Ko-fi, and FEEDBACK
GRAPH & HISTORY
Tap GRAPH in the footer to view your history with trend lines per stat
Use date range filters (7D / 14D / 30D / 3M / 6M / 12M / ALL) — swipe the filter row if needed
Log a reading for a past date using the date picker
Tap EDIT on a reading to reload its values into the sliders
Tap DEL to permanently delete a reading
Notes are shown under each entry in the history list — over months, notes may become the most valuable part of the app. The graph shows what. The note shows why.
History shows the last 7 entries — tap SHOW MORE to see older readings
Tap ↗ SHARE to generate a clean read-only page for the current date range — useful for medical appointments
Trends show patterns over time — not precise measurement. This app shows what you logged. It doesn't tell you what to think.
SETTINGS
Toggle ADVANCED MODE at the top of settings to access additional options
→ Simple mode shows the essentials. Advanced unlocks stat names, slider increment, and log time settings.
Set your personal baseline in Preferences
→ Condition thresholds adjust relative to your centre. Suggested baselines update condition thresholds and drift detection — they do not move your sliders.
Add a note to self
→ Displayed on the main HUD as a grounding anchor
Set your timezone — tap AU in the footer to open directly
→ Updates the clock display
Tap the ◉ percentage next to text size to enter a value directly (80–140)
Tap the version number to view the full changelog
Set a daily check-in reminder via Google Calendar
Enable Colourblind mode for a high-contrast palette
Baseline is personal and does not represent a universal standard.
EXPORT / RESTORE
BACKUP ALL DATA exports your full history and settings as JSON
RESTORE FROM BACKUP imports a saved backup
→ Recommended before updating versions
Export as CSV for use in Excel or Google Sheets
↗ SHARE AS IMAGE creates a visual snapshot of your current status
All data is stored locally in your browser. Export regularly to avoid loss.
